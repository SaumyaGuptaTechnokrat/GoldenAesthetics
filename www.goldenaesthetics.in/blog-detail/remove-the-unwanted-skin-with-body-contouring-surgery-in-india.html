What is Body Contouring?

As embraced above, a body contouring procedure is carried out to remove unwanted fat or skin and reshape your body to get a well-defined look and enhance your personality. It is commonly referred to as the body sculpting procedure that eliminates excess fat, helps in shaping the areas of the body and also tightens the loose skin.

Body contouring does not help you in losing weight, but it helps in reshaping your body curves.

Body contouring is carried for the following procedures:

1. Arm Lift - It is also known as Brachioplasty, is one of the cosmetic surgery procedures carried out to reduce excessive fat and loose skin on the underside of the arm between the armpit and the elbow. It is mainly carried out to create a slim and toned look of the upper arm. If you feel uncomfortable and not satisfied with the sagging of the arm skin, then you should follow the arm lift procedure.

2. Lower Body Lift - The lower body lift involves the removal of unwanted fat and tissue from the lower abdomen as well as reshaping and tightening the skin of your thighs, buttocks, and flanks. Lower body lifts are generally considered to be linked to psychological benefits, including an improved sense of body image.

3. Upper Body Lift - It is carried out to remove excess skin of the body area that extends to the side of the upper back. Once you undergo the treatment for upper lift, you will get rid of skin folds and rolls on the upper back and also under the arms.

4. Thigh Lift - The thigh lift can help you by reducing unwanted skin as well as fat from the thigh area, resulting in smoother skin.

5. Breast Lift - It is also known as breast augmentation which can help restore the shape of the breast or remove unwanted fat and sagging skin after pregnancy and breastfeeding.

Why is Body Contouring Required ?

As described in the above section, there may have been some stubborn fat deposits and extra skin on your body parts if you have lately dropped a substantial amount of weight. Nowadays body contouring is gaining importance as many people want to live a better lifestyle and achieve a toned appearance. Body contouring removes stubborn fat that is quite difficult to remove by doing even excessive exercises. This process can provide you with a successful long-term fix. 

Here are the reasons described to highlight the importance of body contouring:

1. Body contouring can be an important requirement due to fat deposits that are unevenly distributed on the body areas such as the thighs, arms, stomach and in such a manner.

2. After giving birth, most women put on extra weight, especially in the abdomen and buttocks area. In such cases too, women can look forward to body contouring or any other form of reshaping that will give them a pleasing appearance and enhance their personality with a well-defined look.

3. The most popular reason for body contouring treatment is obesity. Overweight people who lose weight may begin to seem slack and droop over their entire body. They can also get the finest cosmetic results for the long term by getting a lower body lift or body contouring.

We find ourselves sometimes very disappointed and odd looking due to obesity and sagging skin, especially in the areas of buttocks, underside arms, abdomen area and so forth. Sometimes, we are also unable to remove unwanted skin or fat by performing strenuous exercises. For all these reasons, body contouring is considered one of the most beneficial treatments to remove excess fat and get rid of loose skin.

1. Minimal Invasive Treatment - Body Contouring in India is a minimally invasive treatment that helps you in reducing fat. This treatment may require oral medication for a short time after the surgery and give better results in the long term.

Reduction of Excessive Fat Pockets - With treatment of Body Contouring from the Best Plastic Surgery Hospital in India can help in the reduction of fat deposits if you follow strict diet schedules and healthy food after the treatment. The stubborn regions under the upper arms and the lower belly can be corrected with an armed life and also by stomach tuck.

4. Improved Comfort - By undergoing the treatment of body contouring, you can feel much better, as excessive or unwanted fat creates difficulty while walking, jumping and in such a manner. 

5. Targeting multiple areas at one time - The surgeon can perform body contouring on more than one area of the body. In the case of lower body lifts, it can remove fat from the thighs and midsection at one time.
