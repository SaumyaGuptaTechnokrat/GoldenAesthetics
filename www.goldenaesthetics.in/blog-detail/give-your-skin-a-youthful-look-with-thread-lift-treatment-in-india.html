Give Your Skin a Youthful Look With Thread Lift Treatment in India

By : Admin

Posted: 2023-01-17

Ageing is a natural process that is inevitable and keeps on going with the passage of your age. But your skin can be rejuvenated by improving the saggy skin with the help of Thread Lift Treatment in India that is well recognised for lifting your skin by tightening it.

This modern world insists you to look best and to enhance your beauty in various different manners. Facial beauty matters a lot in representing your personality traits and also sagging skin can be a drawback that can decrease your confidence in case when your face plays an important role in your profession. Not only in profession but also in personal life there could be certain occasions where you lack your confidence and feel embarrassed due to sagging skin. 

Every problem always comes with its solution. Similarly, your skin texture can be improved by lifting your skin and also by eliminating sagging skin with the aid of thread lift treatment.  

What is Thread Lift Treatment ?

Thread Lift Treatment is a non-surgical procedure that is done for producing a subtle but visible lift in your skin with the application of temporary sutures in order to reshape the saggy skin. This treatment does not involve the removal of saggy facial skin surgically but involves medical-grade threads that are inserted inside the target area of your skin that is useful in pulling up your skin. This treatment pulls the skin up that leads to lifting and tightening your face. 

Thread lift treatment indirectly and logically lifts your face by stimulating the healing response of your skin as with the entrance of any foreign particle in your skin, your skin will automatically act against it by self defending. Thus, inserted thread fights against ageing in such a manner that the body stimulates the facial skin to act against thread by producing collagen in large amounts in treated areas. Collagen is well known for playing a vital role in the ageing process. 

Collagen is a protein that improves the strength, elasticity and flexibility of your skin naturally. Thread lift treatment gradually improves your skin's tone and elasticity.

Thread Lift Procedures 

There are mainly two different types of procedures used for thread lift treatment:

1. Nova Threads

2. Silhouette InstaLift

1. Nova Threads 

Nova Threads are the sutures that are mainly prepared from biocompatible material known as Polydioxanone (PDO) that is considered safe for reabsorption by the body. Nova Threads takes about four to six months after the treatment to dissolve in skin. Nova Threads provide long lasting results for about a year or more than 1 year by improving the condition of your skin due to collagen infusion.

There are different forms of Nova Threads namely Barbed Sutures and Curved or Straight Smooth Sutures. Barbed sutures are mostly preferred for more skin lift action whereas smooth sutures are preferred for more collagen production with less lift action.

Nova Threads is a quick and easy procedure that mostly takes about 30 to 45 minutes to complete the whole treatment. The procedure will be started by giving you local anaesthesia to numb the target areas then a small tool, namely cannula or needle, will be applied to insert the threads inside the skin that will help to lift your skin by tightening the skin.

2. Silhouette InstaLift

Silhouette InstaLift, like Nova Thread, uses biocompatible temporary sutures to lift your face. Silhouette InstaLift is mainly concerned with face lifting and less concerned with collagen production but performs both the functions collectively. The threads used for this procedure are designated differently from others. These threads mainly contain a number of sutures distributed with minute cones that function by grabbing skin tissues proficiently. 

This procedure shows a huge noticeable difference in skin lifting by gathering more skin at once. Silhouette InstaLift threads provide more long lasting results for about one to three years. The whole procedure takes about approximately 45 minutes to get completed. 

Silhouette InstaLift procedure starts with local anaesthesia to avoid pain and discomfort then a needle, that is particularly designed for skin lifting, is used to insert the thread into the skin. 

In both the procedures, you can continue with your normal routine and activities immediately after the treatment. 

Difference Between Thread Lift and Facelift 

In most cases, you may usually misunderstand the exact difference between the thread lift treatment and face lift surgery as the main purpose of both surgeries are similar. Therefore, it may be confusing sometimes whether both the treatments are similar or not. Here you will get the precise solution of this confusion by knowing the difference between the thread lift and facelift. 

  • Thread lift is a non-surgical procedure that uses the medical grade threads to lift the skin whereas Facelift is a surgical procedure that is done by making incisions on the face and does not involve the use of threads.

  • Thread lift treatment does not require deep cuts or incisions, only threads are inserted inside the skin that lift the skin. On the other hand, in facelift surgery, there is a requirement of deep incisions at target areas of your face that can be recovered later after surgery. 

  • Thread lift treatment can last for about one to three years depending upon the type of thread used but facelift surgery has more long lasting results than threadlift. 

  • In thread lift treatment you will be given with local anaesthesia only whereas facelift surgery requires general or heavier anaesthesia.

  • Thread lift treatment recovers much earlier than the facelift lift surgery (it take more time to heal wounds or incisions)

  • Thread Lift Treatment For Face is mostly recommended to the people with age groups between 30 to 50 as it deals with moderate signs of ageing whereas facelift surgery deals with prominent and more noticeable signs of ageing and recommended for people with age group of 30 to 65 years old. 

  • Risks or complications in thread lift treatment are less than that of facelift surgery.

Last but not the least, consult specialists of the Best Plastic Surgery Hospital in India for safe and trusted procedures for thread lift treatment for the face. 

Areas Suitable for Thread Lift

Following are the areas of your face where thread lift can be easily treated:

  • Forehead

  • Eyebrows

  • Jowls

  • Jawline or chin 

  • Neck 

  • Under eye area

Benefits of Thread Lift Treatment 

Thread Lift Treatment carries several benefits that insists you to have this treatment. The important benefits of thread lift treatment are given below: 

Earlier Recovery

Thread Lift treatment involves less invasive procedure and also requires local anaesthesia that naturally leads to very soon as well as easy recovery. Medication is also not required for recovery, you may get back to your work immediately after the treatment.

No Scarring 

Since, thread lift treatment does not include deep incisions thus, there are no chances of scarring.

Less Complications

In thread lift treatment, you are given with local anaesthesia that reduces the chances of side effects or complications. With thread lift treatment, you will be at very low risk of side effects.

Cost-Effective 

The average cost of thread lift treatment is approximately 40% less than that of the cost of traditional facelift. Therefore, thread lift is more affordable for you. 

Side Effects of Thread Lift Treatment

Like all other non-surgical treatments, thread lift also has some side effects but upto very low extent as follows: 

  • Bleeding due to insertion of needle into your skin.

  • Dimpling may be visible at places of insertion.

  • Little pain or discomfort due to tightly placed threads.

  • Allergic infections.

  • Swelling.

These complications can be easily treatable and can be recovered in very less time.
