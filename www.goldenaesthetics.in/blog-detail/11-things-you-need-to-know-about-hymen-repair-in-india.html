11 Things You Need to Know about Hymen Repair in India

By : Admin

Posted: 2022-02-09

Do you know what is hymen repair? Hymenoplasty, also known as hymenoplasty, hymen reconstruction, hymen surgery or hymen repair surgery in India is a surgical procedure designed to restore or rebuild the hymen. This is a hymenoplasty procedure to rebuild the hymen, a pink membrane designed to block the entrance to the vagina. Hymenoplasty or hymen repair surgery is primarily undertaken by women before marriage, especially those who condone premarital sex.  

Let’s discuss the 11 things you need to know about Hymenoplasty:

1. Types 

There are two different types of reconstruction, repair, depending on the type of hymen and the shape of the tear. These types include

  •  Temporary hymen surgery 
  •  Permanent hymen surgery 

For temporary reconstruction or repair of the hymen; what is done is a repair, a corrective procedure. Permanent hymen surgery is also known as long term repair or permanent repair.     

2. Who Can Opt For Hymenoplasty?

A simple surgical procedure of Hymenoplasty is designed to rebuild the hymen after it has ruptured, returning it to what some call a "pre-sexual" state. Hence, it is opted by many women who believe that it can be used to restore their virginity.     

3. Can Hymenoplasty Guarantees Virginity?

It is unreasonable to think that a person can restore or gain virginity through hymenoplasty. Hymenoplasty is a method of restoring the integrity of a torn hymen, so signs of the hymen can be assumed. 

4. Reasons for Ruptured Hymen

A woman’s hymen can break while riding a horse or cycling, but the social constructs surrounding virginity seem to depend on keeping it intact. Also, the hymen ruptures during a woman's first intercourse, but in some cases, the use of a tampon or vigorous exercise can also cause the hymen to rupture without the woman's knowledge. Hymen tissue can cause pain and bleeding when it breaks during intercourse, but some women can have sex without tearing, so this is not a clear sign of virginity. An intact hymen can make it difficult (if not impossible) to use a tampon or have painless vaginal intercourse.     

5. Why is it Done?

If a person has a disease that affects the hymen, surgery is usually required. Hymen reconstruction surgery is an extremely delicate procedure performed for a variety of personal and/or religious reasons, which may include the desire to tear the hymen during intercourse on the patient's wedding night.
This procedure is becoming increasingly popular as recently women have chosen to undergo hymen reconstruction surgery for personal reasons such as religious or cultural conformity, psychological healing, trauma healing, or re-virginity. Hymen repair surgery in India is the cosmetic repair, restoration, or creation of a woman's hymen, and women often request it for physical, psychological, or cultural reasons.     

6. How is Hymen Repair Done? 

In the hymenoplasty surgical procedure, the surgeon creates a new hymen using tissue from the vaginal lip. And, in the Vaginoplasty approach, the surgeon tightens the vaginal tissue to create a healed hymen. Both of these alternatives do not provide a non-ruptured hymen, but you will get a tight entrance to the vagina.     

If there is not enough skin to repair the hymen, or if there is no hymen, the surgeon can create one using a piece of thin vaginal skin (the vaginal mucosa) or synthetic tissue. Bleeding occurs when the hymen ruptures, which is usually the result of a woman's first intercourse, so this type of surgery fuses the tissues to restore the "virgin" quality. 

Any torn skin along the edges of the hymen is carefully and gently cut, after which the remaining tissue is sewn together, leaving a small opening. With the help of a surgical technique, the torn edges of the hymen are joined together.     

During hymen reconstruction surgery, our expert doctors will pull together the torn edges of the hymen and stitch them back together. Then the surgeon sews the torn parts together - the sutures dissolve.  

7. How Much Time Does Hymenoplasty Surgery in Amritsar Take?

This hymenoplasty takes about two hours and you will be under local anesthesia. The procedure of Hymenoplasty is very simple and involves rebuilding the hymen with thin dissolvable sutures. 

8. Is Hymenoplasty Surgery Painful? 

The surgery is completely painless as it is performed under local anesthesia. You can also sleep with a sedative during the procedure if you are concerned about the procedure. 

9. Recovery Period after Hymenoplasty

Usually, no hospital stay is required after this surgery and you can get back to normal life the very next day but in order to get completely fine, you should rest for about 5-6 weeks. 

There may be some bleeding during the first 48-72 hours, but this is completely normal. 

10. Do You Get Natural Results After the Surgery?

After this type of hymen surgery and without complications, subsequent intercourse usually ruptures the membrane, causing pain and bleeding. Bleeding after hymenoplasty is the same as with a natural hymen. This type of situation includes operations usually performed by stitching the remnants of the hymen.     

The operation performed does not create the hymen as before, the purpose of the flap method is already to form a texture that allows bleeding in this area. When the hymen only has a small opening (micro-perforated hymen), surgery may be used to relieve pain and discomfort.     

11. Who Can Perform the Hymenoplasty?

If the repair of the hymen will be done by temporary methods, both plastic surgeons and gynecologists can do so. For example, in the event that the hymen is torn off due to the insertion of a tampon, medical device, or another way, a repair technician can simply sew the remaining pieces together. As far as permanent repairs go, yes, even a few times, the hymen can be rebuilt.
