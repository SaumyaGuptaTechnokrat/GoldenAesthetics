Transplantation of hair is a surgical process in which the hair follicles are taken from other parts of the body and implanted in the area of baldness on the body. Hair transplantation can also be used for the rehabilitation of body hair growth such as eyelashes, eyebrows, and beard hair, and to fill in scars caused by accidents. 

In hair transplantation, the follicles taken from the body are called the donor part, and to which part the follicles are to be implemented is known as the recipient part of the body. This medical technique in case of surgical hair transplant involves the punch grafts to remove the hair from the full thickness of the hair growth area with a circular punch from the donor part that is the hair-bearing scalp and transferring in larger numbers to the bald area or the affected part of the body with thinning of hair and baldness. This medical clinic uses the PRP hair technique for Hair Transplant in Amritsar.

What is PRP Hair Technique?

PRP is a medical term especially recognized as the best hair treatment for transplantation of hair which follows a special trio of steps. In this technique, a patient’s blood is drawn from the affected air of thinning hair and the area of baldness, the blood taken is then processed and injected into the scalp. For more detail, read the steps given below:

Step1 - Firstly, the blood is taken with a syringe from your arm and regulated in the machine that spins very fastly and separates fluids of different densities.

Step2 - Secondly, after the centrifugal process the blood sample taken for testing is separated into three segments such as:

  • Platelet poor plasma 

  • Platelet-rich plasma 

  • Red blood cells 

Step3 - Thirdly, the segment, platelet-rich plasma is drawn and processed under scientific modifications that is beneficial for increasing hair growth. It is then injected into the affected areas of the scalp. 

Uses of PRP Hair Transplant:

  • Doctors mostly recommend PRP as the best hair transplant technique as it helps in healing the affected areas and also in restoration of the hair growth.

  • This hair treatment improves the growth of damaged hair follicles.

  • It increases blood supply to the follicles resulting in enhancing and thickening of existing hair follicles.

Facts about the PRP Hair Treatment:

1. Early Times: PRP therapy has been used for a long time since the 1980s. PRP was earlier used for muscle, tissue regeneration, wound healing, alopecia, and so forth. Hair treatment, it is used from the year 2006 to treat hair follicles for increased growth of the hairs on the affected areas of baldness.

2. Non-Invasive Surgery: PRP treatment is a non-surgical treatment in which only an injection or syringe is used for drawing the blood from the arm and then centrifuging it, in which three layers of blood are separated and the platelet-rich plasma is drawn and injected into the scalp.

3. Painless Procedure: This treatment is firmly painless and sometimes doctors may advise anaesthesia when required or requested for hair follicle treatment.

4. Recovery Period: Hair growth may take up to 5-6 weeks depending on the severity of the baldness.

5. No Use of Medications: This treatment does not involve any oral medication or any type of topical creams during and after PRP treatment. It is very rare that skin infection occurs, in that case, patients are advised to use antibiotics.

6. No Hospitalisation: PRP treatment is a usual treatment. Hence, there is no need for hospitalisation.

7. Realistic Results: Such hair treatment reduces hair fall and increases the growth of hairs naturally.

8. No Transmitted Diseases: The blood drawn during the treatment is taken from the patient’s body so there is no risk of treatment rejection or disease transmission.

9. Avoid Harmful Substances: Avoiding For increasing hair growth, hard dyes or chemical substances should be avoided at least 48 hours from the treatment.
