Get a Well - Defined Face Look with Beard Transplant in Amritsar

Posted: 2022-10-18

Have you been suffering from beard problems since your teens? Or you met some accident that caused you to suffer from beard problems? If yes, then you know very well that the need for a Beard Transplant in Punjab arises as many of you desire a long and thick beard to give an attractive look to your face. But, this only happens when your beard issues are solved. And, the problems sometimes are not solved by medications, home remedies or other precautions. 

The only permanent solution to such a problem is a beard transplant which you can avail yourself of from the best clinic for Hair Transplant in Amritsar, Golden Aesthetics. A surgery only knows the exact problem and can fix it by giving permanent solutions and relief for a lifetime. Here is information in detail to let you know about beard restoration before undergoing the surgery:

Beard Transplant

Beard transplant or restoration is a surgical procedure that allows you to have a long and thick beard by transplanting hair from one part to another of the body like the chest, thighs, and so on. However, the surgical procedure is not a serious thing as it becomes common in today’s times. The surgery is performed under local anaesthesia and is reliable, easy and painless. 

The surgery takes long hours like three to eight hours to execute because each graft of hair is transplanted accurately by using methods of hair transplant called FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction) and FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation) depending on the patient's condition or medical background.

Reasons to Opt for Beard Restoration

  • A beard transplant is a process similar to a hair transplant on the head or any other part of the body. In this, the surgeon chooses the right hair that consistently matches your facial hair and transplants it to the affected area. 

  • Beard Transplant is beneficial for those men who are having problems with the growth of their beard due to genetics. But fortunately, anyone can have a beard transplant to replicate the appearance of the natural hair growth on the face.

  • Due to technological advancement, the healing process has become faster than any other surgical operation. You can get back to work after the transplant within two or three days.

  • Beard Transplant is a painless session. Moreover, we can add that it is a non-invasive surgery.

  • Beard transplant involves enhancing the existing beard or constructing a new beard by transplanting scalp hair to the affected area.

  • It can be carried out to hide permanent scars caused by accidents or injuries.Men with facial hair are generally appraised as mature and attractive. 

  • Moreover, a successful beard transplant gives a fuller and more natural look as the hair follicles taken from the scalp match the hair colour of the beard too which makes the beard look natural.

  • Beard Transplant is performed based on outpatient care, that is you can go home after the completion of the procedure.Other than this, doctors avoid general anaesthesia and use local anaesthesia for donor and recipient areas.

 Note: Last but not the least, it is the most permanent solution for hair loss for patchy beards.

Process of Beard Transplant

Beard Transplant is almost similar to a hair transplant in which there is a relocation of hair follicles from one donor to the recipient area. In beard transplant, the hair follicles are taken from the scalp and then transplanted into the affected area by using FUE and FUT techniques. For beard growth, men can choose any hair pattern for the beard they want to grow. Golden Aesthetics Clinic leads as the best Beard transplant in Punjab. Beard Transplant can be performed by two most preferred techniques such as:

  1. Follicular Unit Extraction - It is abbreviated as the FUE and is considered the most common beard transplant method. During the transplantation, the surgeon takes a cluster of hair follicles either from the side of the scalp or from the back of the scalp from the donor area and relocates those hair follicles in the recipient area. The hair follicles are transplanted using small incisions in the affected area. It is the most commonly performed non-invasive surgery that is less painful. Such men are generally suggested for FUE those who are looking for short hair. Moreover, this technique of beard transplant shows a fast recovery and is less painful, leaving no scars and rare chances of getting any type of side effects.

  2. Follicular Unit Transplantation - It is another most preferred technique after Follicular Unit Extraction. This technique is usually recommended for those men who are looking for long hair. It is painful as it involves strip harvesting. In this, a small strip of the tissue is removed from the scalp and then the strip is dissected into smaller sections of follicular grafts. The strip taken from the donor area creates a wound that is stitched afterwards leaving a scar on the donor area. The scar fades away slowly after a long time but it does not disappear completely.

Comparison Between FUE and FUT

  1. Follicular Unit Extraction is recommended for those men who need short hairs and is less painful whereas Follicular Unit Transplantation is recommended for those men who need long hairs and is more painful than follicular unit extraction.

  2. Follicular Unit Extraction hides the scar caused by accidents or injuries whereas follicular unit transplantation leaves a scar during operation that becomes less visible but does not disappear completely.

Tools Used During Beard Transplant

Golden Aesthetics conducts surgeries for Beard Transplant in Amritsar that involves a varied use of operation tools such as local anaesthesia, small incisions, micro-punch, sapphire blade, treatment chair, DHI Implanter Pen and so forth.

  • Local Anaesthesia - A beard transplant is a non-invasive or less painful surgery. Experts use local anaesthesia only to ensure the painless transplantation of grafts from donor to recipient area. 

  • Sharp Tools - Sapphire Blade is mostly used in FUE hair transplantation. It is used to open smaller micro channels in areas where the grafts are removed from the donor area. It involves minimal bleeding as the sapphire blade is ultra-sharp and makes a fine cut on the donor area.

  • Punch Tool - Micro punch is a drill or small needle that is used to remove the grafts. These instruments are generally 0.6 mm to 1 mm in diameter. 

  • Treatment Chair - The transplantation process takes about 4-6 hours and for the comfort of the patient, a high quality and comfortable chair is required.

  • Petri Dish - It is used to place the hair follicles after removing them from the donor area. Petri dish contains a special substance that helps the hair follicles to survive outside the head so that they are kept alive until they are relocated to the recipient area.

  • DHI Implanter Pen - It is a pen-like instrument with a needle. The surgeon takes out grafts one by one with the help of this needle.
